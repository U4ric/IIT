Project Idea


Overview

I wish to implement a piece of software in the form of an App for mobiles and Desktop computer giving the user the ability to get their music played in Nightclubs directly, among other capabilities, with a pass in rigorous screening processes for quality.

Motivation

My Project idea would have to come from an industry that I understand a little more in depth, and that is the Music Industry. Being a DJ as a weekend job, brought me many connections and while my brief time as a Music Producer and still having access to the software and hardware to create said music, there were certain tools I wish had been around for ease the release schedules

Description

My idea would exist in the form of a Mobile App and Desktop counterpart to reach out to as many devices as possible. I yearn for the capability of the Producers music being played directly by DJs in Nightclubs. Tracks that have gone through a screening process by a team of professionals for Stereo Imaging, uniqueness and excitability would make the cut as an example. Every time the DJ would play the track, the Producer would directly get royalties for each play as well as the DJ taking a percentage of the funds. The model would be subscription based with offerings for yearly discounts to keep the App maintained and company afloat. The software would read that the track has been played by either the controller on the Deck (DJ control device), connecting the mobile wirelessly or wired to the Deck itself, thus resulting in the Software knowing that its version is being streamed or used to the device. The App would have the capability to download the tracks if a poor streaming connection is foreseen. The App/Desktop version would integrate Vocalists, Musicians and Producers with the ability to collaborate on Productions together through a separate section within the software. The same goes for live gigs as I would implement an area for people to list their parties, nightclubs to list their gigs and major events to be listed, all verified of course. This is a way of bringing the whole Music community together and easing the life of a freelancer.

Tools and Technologies

The programming code needed to create the software would be along the lines of Java, JavaScript, C, C++, C#, Swift and Python. The hardware devices would be Android and IOS devices, covering PC and Mac systems.

Skills Required

Design Teams, Software Developers, Administration, I.T. Helpdesk Support, Sales Teams, Assistants, Quality Control, Managers and Marketing teams to maintain the company on a large scale. Design teams would need to draft an idea around the UI and layout of the software, including the color scheme. A complex understanding of programming, testing, troubleshooting would be needed to get the software built from the ground up. Administration would be running all the duties and interfacing with the client’s needs. Helpdesk will be required to tend to any Hardware related I.T. problems as well as tending to customers’ needs in terms of technical support. Managers and Marketing teams will be responsible in advertising the product for consumer use.

Outcome

With the success of the project in the long term, I would like to take the software to infinite scalability, investing in more company solutions, workplace stress-free environments for the employees and where the business becomes a household name. Software that Audio Enthusiasts worldwide will thoroughly be satisfied and forever grateful for creating a piece of Technology that revolutionized the Industry. A piece of software that brought ease of use and peace of mind to supporting the dreams of anyone who wanted to make Music their full-time career and was told it was not feasible. Someone that will settle for nothing less than their passion.
